Financial support for graduate students and young researchers
 

We are happy to announce that some financial support is available for travelling to RECOMB 2003.

ISCB is awarding travel fellowships for students that are ISCB members.
Please have a look at http://www.iscb.org/travel_fellowship_form.php.

The European Commission provides financial support to RECOMB 2003 to encourage the participation of young researchers.

Candidates for the EU grants should 

  • play an active role in RECOMB 2003, by at least presenting her / his own results
  • be younger than or maximum 35 years at the time of RECOMB 2003 (April 10-13, 2003). Allowance will be made for compulsory military or civil service (actual time spent in military or civil service) and childcare (maximum 2 years per child for the actual time spent off work).
  • be nationals of a Member State of the European Union or an Associated State, and active inside a Member State or an Associated State at the time of RECOMB 2003. The Associated States are Bulgaria, Cyprus, the Czech Republic, Estonia, Hungary, Latvia, Lithuania, Malta, Poland, Romania, Slovakia, Slovenia, Norway, Iceland, Liechtenstein, Israel and Switzerland.
Please contact beziat@molgen.mpg.de.

RECOMB itself will be able to award travel fellowships for students travelling from the US. Please contact beziat@molgen.mpg.de.

DEADLINE  FOR ALL GRANTS IS JANUARY 13, 2003.

Preference for all grants will be given to students who present their work at the conference (papers or poster). For application, please send by email a short (1 page) description to to the adresses mentioned above, including

Name
Date of birth
Nationality
Affiliation
Ph.D. Advisor Name (in case of graduate students)
Expected graduation date
A short description of your research area and your work
Are you presenting a paper at RECOMB 2003?
Are you presenting a poster at RECOMB 2003?

Please notice that only one of the alternatives above can be chosen.
